Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying and selling on WatchBazaar PK.

Buying on WatchBazaar

Placing an order on WatchBazaar is simple:

  1. Browse our collection and select a watch you like
  2. Click "Add to Cart" or "Make an Offer" for negotiable items
  3. Proceed to checkout and fill in your shipping details
  4. Choose your preferred payment method
  5. Complete the payment and wait for your watch to arrive!

Yes! Many listings on WatchBazaar accept offers. Look for the "Make an Offer" button on product pages. You can submit your offer and the seller will respond within 48 hours. Sellers can accept, reject, or counter your offer.

We offer a 7-day return policy for all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your watch, you can return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the watch is in its original condition. Return shipping costs may apply depending on the reason for return.

Yes! We support in-person meetings for transactions. During checkout, you can select "In-Person Meeting" as your delivery method. Both parties will agree on a safe meeting location. We recommend meeting at public places like malls or authorized dealer showrooms.

Selling on WatchBazaar

To become a seller on WatchBazaar:

  1. Create a buyer account first
  2. Go to "Become a Seller" in your dashboard
  3. Fill out the seller application form
  4. Submit required documents (CNIC, business proof if applicable)
  5. Wait for approval (usually 24-48 hours)

WatchBazaar charges a competitive commission on successful sales:
  • Standard Sellers: 5% commission
  • Verified Sellers: 3.5% commission
  • Premium Sellers: 2.5% commission
Listing watches is free. You only pay when you make a sale.

New listings are typically reviewed within 24 hours. Our team verifies the information, images, and pricing before making your listing live. Premium and verified sellers may enjoy faster approval times.

Payments

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:
  • Bank Transfer (All major Pakistani banks)
  • JazzCash
  • EasyPaisa
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Cash on Delivery (for orders under Rs. 100,000)

Sellers receive payment within 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt of the watch, or automatically 7 days after delivery if no issues are reported. Payments are transferred directly to your registered bank account.

Shipping & Delivery

Delivery times vary by location:
  • Major Cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad): 2-3 business days
  • Other Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days
You'll receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.

We have a multi-layer authentication process:
  • All sellers must provide documentation (box, papers, receipts)
  • High-value watches undergo expert verification
  • We partner with certified watch experts for authentication
  • Our buyer protection covers you if authenticity issues arise

Account & Security

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires in 24 hours for security purposes.

We recommend these security practices:
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Never share your login credentials
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Review your account activity regularly
